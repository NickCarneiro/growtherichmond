YIMBYTown 2017 Scholarship

What starts in San Francisco changes the world-- and our neighborhood is no exception! This summer, local students will have a rare opportunity to learn about the state-of-the-art in urban planning.

On July 13-18th, Grow The Richmond will sponsor two students to attend the YIMBYTown conference in Oakland.

Attendees will learn about urbanism from authors, academics, activists, and policymakers from around the world.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be currently enrolled in high school or college
  • Must live in The Richmond District

The conference will provide meals and the winning students will be given a small transit per diem.
